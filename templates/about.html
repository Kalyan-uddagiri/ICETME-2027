Discover Tripura

Incredible Tripura

Nestled in the lush landscapes of Northeast India, Tripura is a region of breathtaking natural beauty and deep cultural heritage. From magnificent palaces to ancient rock carvings, the state offers a unique blend of history and tranquility.

Delegates will experience the warm hospitality, vibrant traditions, and serene environment that makes Tripura an inspiring backdrop for global academic discourse.

history_edu

Rich Royal History

Ruled by the illustrious Manikya dynasty for centuries, Tripura boasts a glorious past recorded in the historical chronicle, the Rajmala. It seamlessly blended ancient tribal traditions with royal Bengali culture before officially integrating into the Indian Union in 1949.

explore

Iconic Destinations

The state is home to majestic architecture and nature. Must-visit sites include the brilliant white Ujjayanta Palace, the breathtaking Neermahal (Eastern India's only water palace), and the colossal ancient rock-cut carvings of Unakoti.

festival

Vibrant Festivals

Tripura's culture comes alive during massive celebrations like Kharchi Puja and Garia Puja. Visitors can witness vibrant indigenous celebrations, featuring colorful ethnic attire and rhythmic traditional performances like the famous Hojagiri dance.

tour

Tourist Spots

Explore the breathtaking attractions and cultural heritage sites that make Tripura a must-visit destination for conference delegates.
