Join the Department of Mechanical Engineering at NIT Agartala for the First International Conference on Emerging Trends in Mechanical Engineering. We invite you to join our global discourse in hybrid mode during 19–21 February 2027.

Registration Fees

The registration fee covers participation in Technical Sessions, Conference kit, Lunch, and Tea/Coffee.

  • Indian students/scholars (Early bird) – Rs. 4000/-
  • Indian students/scholars (after Jan 10, 2027) – Rs. 5000/-
  • International students/scholars (Early bird) – $100
  • International students/scholars (after Jan 10, 2027) – $150
  • Indian academicians (Early bird) – Rs. 7000/-
  • Indian academicians (after Jan 10, 2027) – Rs. 8000/-
  • International academicians (Early bird) – $200
  • International academicians (after Jan 10, 2027) – $300
  • Industry participants from India (Early bird) – Rs. 10,000/-
  • Industry participants from India (after Jan 10, 2027) – Rs. 11,000/-
  • Industry participants from outside India (Early bird) – $300
  • Industry participants from outside India (after Jan 10, 2027) – $400

group Group Benefit

1 complimentary registration is provided for groups of 5 participants registering from the same affiliation.

library_books Additional Papers

An individual participant submitting an additional paper will receive a 25% waiver on the registration fee for each subsequent paper.

** Note: Registration Fees are non-refundable under any circumstances.

Please follow the three-step process below to successfully submit your manuscript through CMT, complete your fee payment, and finalize your registration for the conference.

upload_file
Step 1

Initial Paper Submission

All authors are required to submit their full papers electronically through the official Microsoft CMT (Conference Management Toolkit) portal. Please ensure your manuscript adheres to the conference formatting guidelines before uploading.

Fee Payment

Upon receiving your paper acceptance confirmation, please proceed to pay the applicable registration fee based on your category listed above. Be sure to save your Transaction ID and payment receipt, as you will need them for the final step.
